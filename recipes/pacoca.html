Paçoca de Amendoim

Description

Paçoca de amndoin (or peanut paçoca in English) is a candy made of ground peanut, sugar and salt. Some recipes also add flour, like cornflour for example. It's a very typical dish mainly present in the states of São Paulo and Minas Gerais.

Ingredients

  • 1,1 lb of unsalted and skinless roasted peanuts
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1/2 of cornflour
  • 1 tablespoon of salt

Steps

  1. Put the peanut, the sugar, the cornflour and the salt in a blender until it gets homogeneous.
  2. Put it in a mold of your choice, modeling with your hands.
  3. Unmold and is ready for consumption
